About Umazen Tools

Free, fast, and reliable TikTok video tools — built for creators and researchers.

🎯 Our Mission

Umazen Tools was created with a simple mission: to provide a fast, free, and genuinely useful set of tools for working with TikTok videos.

Whether you're a content creator backing up your own work, a researcher studying social media trends, a teacher using video clips for educational purposes, or simply someone who wants to save a video for offline viewing — Umazen is built for you.

Our commitment: Keep it simple, keep it free, and keep it honest. No hidden fees, no mandatory sign-ups, no bloated software to install.

🛠️ What We Build

Umazen currently offers two core tools:

⬇️ Video Downloader

Download public TikTok videos in the highest available quality, directly to your device.

🖼️ Frame Extractor

Extract still frames from any video file as PNG images, right in your browser — no upload needed.

Both tools run directly in your browser. The frame extractor processes video locally on your device, meaning your video file never leaves your computer.

💡 Why We Built This

The web is full of TikTok downloader sites loaded with pop-ups, redirects, forced installations, and suspicious ads that expose users to malware. Most of them deliver a poor experience just to squeeze out ad revenue.

We wanted something different — a clean, fast tool that respects users and actually works. Umazen was built from the ground up with these principles:

  • No dark patterns. No fake download buttons, no misleading ads, no forced clicks.
  • No sign-up walls. Use the tool immediately without creating an account.
  • Privacy by default. We don't log your URLs or track your downloads.
  • Mobile-first design. Works great on phones and tablets, not just desktop.
  • Open about limitations. If a download fails, we tell you why.

⚙️ How It Works

When you paste a TikTok URL and click Download, here's what happens:

  • Our server validates and normalises the URL (stripping tracking parameters)
  • We use yt-dlp — a widely-trusted, open-source video extraction library — to fetch the video stream
  • If the primary method is unavailable, we automatically try a fallback method
  • The video is piped directly to your browser download — we do not permanently store video files
  • Temporary files are automatically deleted within minutes

The frame extractor works entirely in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API — your video file is never uploaded to our servers.

Important: This tool works with publicly accessible TikTok videos only. We do not support downloading private videos, and we have no ability to access content that TikTok restricts.

⚖️ Responsible Use

We are committed to operating responsibly. Umazen is designed for legitimate personal and educational use cases:

  • Backing up your own TikTok content
  • Saving videos for offline educational viewing
  • Research and academic study of social media content
  • Fair use commentary, criticism, or parody
  • Extracting frames for design references or storyboarding

We explicitly prohibit using our tools for mass-downloading, commercial redistribution, or any activity that violates TikTok's Terms of Service or applicable copyright law. See our Terms of Service for full details.
