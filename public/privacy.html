🍪 Cookie Policy

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly and provide information to website owners.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Site to function. They enable core features such as theme preferences (dark/light mode). These cannot be disabled.

2. Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on this Site. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites on the Internet. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our Site and/or other sites on the Internet.

How Google AdSense Uses Cookies: Google uses cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve relevant, personalised ads on our Site. These cookies enable Google to recognise your browser and remember certain information about your browsing history.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalised advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our Site, please visit: How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.

3. Analytics Cookies

We may use basic analytics to understand how visitors use our Site (e.g., which pages are most popular, how long people stay). This helps us improve the user experience. Analytics data is aggregated and does not identify individual users.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in the following ways:

  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete existing cookies. See your browser's help section for instructions.
  • Opt out of Google personalised ads: Google Ads Settings
  • Opt out of third-party advertising networks: Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out
  • Opt out of Network Advertising Initiative: NAI Opt-Out

Please note that disabling cookies may affect your experience on this Site and other websites.
