⚡ Common Questions

Before reaching out, check if your question is already answered:

  • Why did my download fail?
    TikTok occasionally blocks cloud servers. Try again after 1–2 minutes, or try a different network. Private or region-restricted videos cannot be downloaded.
  • Is this tool free?
    Yes, completely free. No sign-up required. We're kept alive by kind donations via Buy Me a Coffee.
  • Can I download videos from other platforms?
    Currently Umazen supports TikTok videos only. We may add other platforms in the future.
  • Does the frame extractor work on any video?
    Yes — the frame extractor works on any video file your browser can play (MP4, WebM, MOV, etc.). No upload to our servers required.
  • How do I report a copyright issue?
    Email umazentech@gmail.com with your details. We respond to valid DMCA notices promptly.
